NY Suicide Data

The New York State Government has developed the website data.ny.gov to provide unprecedented access to the state's data. The portal contains information on several categories such as economic development, health, transparency, education, and transportation, among others.

This portal gave STOP Suicide access to relevant information by county and by age group. Follow the links to learn more about the distribution of suicide deaths in New York state.
