Are you having thoughts about suicide?

If you are in a state of distress, reaching out is the first step to safety. Click on these dedicated crisis services websites to get immediate help: Suicide Prevention Lifeline, Veterans Crisis Line, CrisisChat.org.

Are you worried about someone?

If you are worried someone might be at risk, don’t assume they will reach out. Only one in five seeks help. You can encourage them to make that critical first step. Here are some suggestions.

Ask if you can talk in private and open up the conversation: "How are you doing? You haven’t seemed yourself lately. Is everything okay? Is anything bothering you?". Listen to their story and express concern and caring. Encourage them to seek mental health services. Tell them seeking help can take courage, but it’s the smart thing to do.
