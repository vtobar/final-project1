Our mission

Every year suicide claims more lives than war, murder, and natural disasters combined, and yet suicide prevention doesn't get anywhere near the funding given to other leading causes of death. It's up to Walkers like our team to make a difference, and we are!!

Our mission is to promote the understanding and prevention of suicide and support those who have been affected by it.

We envision a world where people know how to prevent suicide and find hope and healing.
