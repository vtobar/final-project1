Connection with Crime

We run a regression of suicides on crime controlling for year and the income (expressed in log).

The coefficients for all the variables are statistically significant at a 1% level. For both crime and income there is a negative relation with the percentage of suicides. It's interesting to notice that the coefficient in income suggests that a 1% increment in income would decrease the percentage of suicides in -0.002 units.
