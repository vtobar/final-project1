Welcome

If you're feeling suicidal, your concerned friends and family members may hesitate to speak up for fear that they're wrong or might say the wrong thing. However, talking openly about suicidal thoughts and feelings can save a life.

Feeling suicidal means that a person has more pain than they feel capable of coping with. Help is available. Don't wait: reach out.

Navigate through this site to understand more about suicide prevention.
